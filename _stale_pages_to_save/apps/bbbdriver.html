BBB Driver

Route management and delivery tracking built for BBB Logistics drivers. Smarter routes, proof of delivery, and real-time customer updates.

Internal tool

The problem it solves

Drivers spend too much time on admin that isn't driving. Route planning, customer updates, and paperwork eat into the day. BBB Driver streamlines it all.

Planned features

  • Smart routing: Optimised delivery routes that adapt to real-time conditions
  • Proof of delivery: Photo capture and digital signatures for every drop-off
  • Customer updates: Automatic notifications so customers know when to expect delivery
  • Earnings tracking: See your day's earnings and mileage at a glance

This is an internal tool being built for Beach to Bridge to Bush Logistics. It's secondary to our main self-quantification app portfolio.
