Account deletion

Request account deletion

Digital ABCs handles deletion requests through the privacy-rights process already published in the Privacy Policy. If you want app account data deleted, contact info@digitalabcs.com.au. Requests are handled subject to legal retention requirements.

How the current process works

This page does not introduce a new workflow. It points to the same deletion-rights path already stated in the Digital ABCs Privacy Policy, where users can request deletion of personal information and app account data.

Current request path

Request deletion

To exercise a deletion request for a Digital ABCs app account, use the published privacy contact email.

  1. Email info@digitalabcs.com.au.
  2. State that you are requesting account deletion for the relevant Digital ABCs app.
  3. Review the Privacy Policy for the retention limits that may still apply in specific legal or operational cases.
Verified in privacy policy

Important notes

  • App account data is retained until account deletion is requested.
  • Deletion requests are subject to legal retention requirements.
  • When data is no longer needed, Digital ABCs states it will securely delete or de-identify it.
