7. Delivery Service Terms

7.1 Pricing

Delivery pricing is based on route distance (fastest route via Google Maps) plus additional stops:

  • 0-20 km: $35 flat rate
  • 20-30 km: $45 flat rate
  • 30-40 km: $55 flat rate
  • 41+ km: $10 base + $2 per kilometre
  • Additional stops: +$8 per stop after the first

Tolls are included in all quoted prices. See our pricing calculator for estimates.

7.2 Booking & Cancellation

  • Bookings should be made at least 24 hours in advance where possible
  • Same-day bookings subject to availability
  • Cancellations within 2 hours of scheduled pickup may incur a $20 cancellation fee
  • No-shows (goods not ready at pickup) may be charged the full delivery fee

7.3 Client Responsibilities - Delivery

You agree to:

  • Ensure goods are properly packaged and labelled before pickup

We do not transport:

  • Illegal substances or contraband

  • We take reasonable care of goods during transport
