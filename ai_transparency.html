AI Transparency Statement

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Digital ABCs builds apps that use AI to solve real problems. We believe you should know exactly when AI is involved, what it's doing, and how your data is handled. No mystery, no "black box" - just straight answers about how our technology works.

1. Our Principles

Human Oversight

Humans Make the Decisions

AI generates suggestions and drafts - humans decide what to use. Every piece of code we ship, every strategy we recommend, gets reviewed by a person. AI is a tool, not the boss.

Data Privacy

Your Data Stays Yours

We don't use your data to train AI models. When we use third-party AI (like Google Gemini), we use enterprise APIs that contractually prevent your data from being used for training. Your conversations, your business data - it's processed and forgotten.

Transparency

Clear About When AI Is Used

If a feature uses AI, we tell you. Before you submit content to an AI-powered feature, you'll know it's being processed by AI. No hidden algorithms working in the background without your knowledge.

Practical Purpose

AI That Actually Helps

We don't use AI because it's trendy. We use it when it genuinely solves a problem better than alternatives. If a simpler solution works, we use that instead.

3. What AI Can't Do

Being honest about AI means being honest about its limitations:

  • AI Can Be Wrong: Large Language Models sometimes confidently state incorrect information ("hallucinations"). We manually verify AI outputs against official sources before using them.
  • AI Lacks Context: AI processes information but doesn't understand your specific business situation. That's why we combine AI analysis with human expertise.
  • AI Can't Replace Judgment: For important decisions, AI provides input - it doesn't make the call. You (and we) always have the final say.
  • AI Reflects Its Training: AI systems can have biases based on their training data. We review AI outputs for fairness and accuracy.

4. Your Control

You Can Always Challenge AI Outputs

If you believe an AI-assisted output in any of our applications or services is incorrect, biased, or problematic, contact us. We'll:

  1. Perform a manual human review
  2. Explain how the output was generated
  3. Correct any errors
  4. Consider improvements to prevent similar issues

Contact: info@digitalabcs.com.au

Opting Out of AI Features

Where possible, we provide alternatives:

  • Some app features can be used without AI processing
  • For consulting services, you can request we avoid AI tools for your project (this may affect pricing)
  • If you have concerns about specific AI usage, discuss it with us - we're flexible

5. Alignment with Australian Standards

Our AI practices align with the Australian Government's Voluntary AI Safety Standard principles:

  • Human oversight: AI assists, humans decide
  • Transparency: Clear communication about AI use
  • Accountability: We take responsibility for AI outputs in our services
  • Privacy protection: Data minimisation and secure handling
  • Fairness: Review for bias and discrimination
  • Contestability: You can challenge AI decisions

6. Changes to Our AI Use

AI technology changes quickly. When we adopt new AI tools or change how we use existing ones, we'll:

  • Update this statement
  • Note significant changes in our changelog
  • Notify users of affected applications about material changes
