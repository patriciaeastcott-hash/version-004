Important

Product limits

Linguistic Decoder is an educational and informational AI-assisted product. The public website preview is even more limited than the full beta and should be treated that way.

  • Use your own judgment and lived context.
  • Start with non-sensitive examples where possible.
  • Do not rely on a beta AI app for safety, abuse, self-harm, or urgent welfare decisions.
