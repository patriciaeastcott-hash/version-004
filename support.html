Important

Product limits

Linguistic Decoder is an educational and informational AI-assisted product. It should be treated as a reflection tool, not a final answer machine.

  • Use your own judgment and lived context.
  • Start with non-sensitive examples where possible.
  • Do not rely on a AI app for safety, abuse, self-harm, or urgent welfare decisions.
