I Build Apps That Fill the Gaps.

When I hit a problem in everyday life and can't find a tool that solves it properly, I build one. Then I put it out there in case it helps someone else too.

I'm Patricia. I'm a mum, I'm neurodivergent, and I notice gaps everywhere - the little things that should be easier but aren't. So I started building apps to fix them. Some are simple calculators. Some are AI-powered tools. All of them exist because they made my life easier, and I figured they might make yours easier too.

Everything here is free to use. If you find something useful, that's the whole point.
