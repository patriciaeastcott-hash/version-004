Studio focus

Digital ABCs is building for adults and children.

The portfolio now sits in two clear lanes: adult-facing self-quantification apps and educational products for children that emphasise reading, cooperation, teamwork, and practical learning support.

Self-quantification for adults

Projects such as Linguistic Decoder and the Compliance Tool are designed for adults who want clearer pattern recognition, better decision support, and more honest product communication.

Educational products for children

Bright Spark and the Minecraft add-ons stream are being shaped as educational tools that support different learning levels while staying grounded in the Australian Curriculum and real home learning contexts.

Founder-led and accountability-first

This is a founder-led studio, not a vague umbrella brand. Current work, known limits, and legal framing are all meant to stay visible so the transparency message is backed by the actual pages people can read.

Transparency is part of the product.

Digital ABCs is building an app studio, not a black box. The legal hub, privacy policy, AI transparency page, terms, child notices, and regional privacy notices are part of the public product surface and should be read that way.
