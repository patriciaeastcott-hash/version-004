In Development

BBB Driver

Route management and delivery tracking built for BBB Logistics. Smarter routes, proof of delivery, and real-time updates - built by a driver, for drivers.

The Problem

Delivery drivers spend too much time on logistics that aren't driving. Manually planning routes, texting customers for updates, taking photos as proof of delivery, then doing paperwork at the end of the day. The actual driving is the easy part - everything around it is the bottleneck.

Existing delivery apps are built for massive courier companies, not independent drivers and small logistics businesses. They're overcomplicated, expensive, and don't fit how we actually work.

What It Will Do

Simple tools for independent drivers and small delivery businesses.

Route Optimisation

Import your delivery list and get the most efficient route. Handles multi-drop runs, time windows, and priority deliveries.

  • Import deliveries from CSV or manual entry
  • Optimised route calculation
  • Turn-by-turn navigation integration
  • Re-route on the fly for changes

Proof of Delivery

Capture photo proof, signatures, and delivery notes directly in the app. No more end-of-day paperwork.

  • Photo capture with timestamp and GPS
  • Digital signature collection
  • Delivery notes and condition reporting
  • Automatic record keeping

Customer Updates

Automatic delivery status updates for your customers. No more "where's my delivery?" phone calls.

  • Automated SMS/email notifications
  • ETA updates in real-time
  • Delivery confirmation alerts
  • Customer delivery tracking link

Driver Dashboard

See your day at a glance. Earnings, kilometres, completed deliveries, and upcoming stops - all in one place.

  • Daily earnings summary
  • Kilometres and fuel tracking
  • Delivery completion stats
  • Weekly/monthly reports for tax time

Development Roadmap

1

Core Route & Delivery Management

Basic route planning, delivery list management, and proof of delivery capture. Getting the fundamentals right.

2

Customer Notifications

Automated delivery status updates via SMS and email. Real-time ETA tracking for customers.

3

Reporting & Analytics

Earnings tracking, mileage logs, and exportable reports for BAS and tax time. Built for Australian sole traders.

4

Business Integration

Connect with BBB Logistics booking system, invoicing tools, and customer management.
