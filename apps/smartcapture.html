Smart Capture Finance

The "black box" recorder for financial workflows. It watches how you process invoices and payments to show you exactly where time and money are being lost.

The Problem

You know your invoicing process is inefficient. You feel it every month when you're chasing payments, fixing errors, or doing the same data entry for the hundredth time. But when someone asks "where exactly is the problem?" - you can't say.

Without hard data, you can't justify spending money to fix it. You can't prove the ROI of automation. You can't even explain to your accountant why it takes so long. So the inefficiency continues, draining your time month after month.

What It Will Do

Like a flight recorder, but for your financial workflows.

Workflow Recording

Captures every step of your invoicing process - the clicks, the wait times, the back-and-forth between applications. All without disrupting how you work.

  • Passive capture - no manual logging
  • Cross-application tracking
  • Time stamps on every action
  • Privacy-first design

Bottleneck Analysis

Processes the recordings to identify exactly where time is being lost. Shows you which steps take longest, where errors occur, and where manual work could be automated.

  • Time-per-step breakdown
  • Error pattern detection
  • Automation opportunity scoring
  • Clear visualisations

Cost Calculation

Translates the time data into actual dollars. Shows you exactly how much your current process is costing and what you'd save by fixing specific bottlenecks.

  • Hourly cost calculations
  • Projected savings estimates
  • ROI for automation investments
  • Evidence for decision-making
