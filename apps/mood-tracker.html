Actionable insights

What you'll be able to do

Daily check-in

Two simple sliders: mood (sad → happy) and energy (exhausted → energised). Add optional tags and a one-line note.

  • Mood slider: How are you feeling?
  • Energy slider: How's your energy?
  • Quick tags: stressed, relaxed, anxious, focused
  • Log multiple times per day

Calendar heatmap

See your entire year at a glance. Spot trends, identify good and bad periods, understand your patterns.

  • Year-at-a-glance view
  • Colour-coded by mood/energy
  • Tap any day to see details

Trend graphs

Line graphs showing mood and energy over time. Weekly, monthly, and yearly views. See your averages by day of week.

  • Mood and energy over time
  • Day-of-week patterns
  • "You're happiest on Saturdays"

Correlation engine

After 30+ days of data, unlock AI-powered insights that reveal what actually affects your wellbeing.

  • Time-lagged effects (late night → bad mood 2 days later)
  • Tag correlations (exercise → higher energy)
  • Weekly patterns (Sunday Scaries)

Simple by design

The faster it is to log, the more likely you'll do it daily. And daily data is what reveals the patterns.
