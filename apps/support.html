Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Linguistic Decoder? ‚ñº
Using Linguistic Decoder is simple: Paste or type any conversation (text, email, chat), select the relationship type and age group for context, and the AI will analyze it instantly. You'll get insights into emotional states, communication goals, and suggestions for how to respond. You can test your response before sending and save analyses for future learning.
What types of conversations can I analyze? ‚ñº
You can analyze any text-based conversation including: work emails, text messages, social media chats, family conversations, relationship discussions, and any other written communication. The app works best with conversations that have emotional or relational complexity where understanding hidden meanings is important.
Is Linguistic Decoder a medical or mental health tool? ‚ñº
No. Linguistic Decoder is an educational AI analysis tool for informational purposes only. It is NOT a substitute for professional mental health services, therapy, counseling, or medical diagnosis. If you're experiencing mental health concerns, depression, anxiety, or any medical condition, please consult with a qualified mental health professional or medical doctor. Linguistic Decoder is designed to help you understand conversations better and improve communication skills, not to diagnose or treat mental health conditions.
Can Linguistic Decoder provide legal or professional advice? ‚ñº
No. While Linguistic Decoder can help you understand conversations better, it is NOT a substitute for professional legal advice, business consulting, or professional guidance. For legal matters, employment issues, contracts, or other professional concerns, please consult with qualified professionals such as lawyers, accountants, or business advisors.
How accurate is the AI analysis? ‚ñº
Linguistic Decoder uses advanced AI to analyze tone, emotion, and communication patterns. However, AI analysis is based on patterns and language analysis‚Äîit's not perfect and should be used as one perspective, not as absolute truth. The app is most accurate when given proper context about the relationship type and situation. Always use your own judgment and consider other perspectives.
Is my conversation data safe and private? ‚ñº
Yes. All conversation data is stored locally on your device‚Äîwe do not store your conversations on our servers. Your data never leaves your phone or device. Linguistic Decoder does not create a history of your analyses or conversation data in the cloud. Your privacy is completely protected.
Can I use suggested responses directly? ‚ñº
The suggested responses from Linguistic Decoder are meant to guide your thinking, not to be used word-for-word in every situation. The app provides diplomatic, assertive, and collaborative options to help you consider different approaches. Always use your own judgment, add your personal voice, and consider what feels authentic to you in the specific situation.
What if Linguistic Decoder misunderstands a conversation? ‚ñº
AI is not perfect. Context matters greatly‚Äîif Linguistic Decoder seems to misunderstand, consider whether you've given it enough context about the relationship type, situation, and history. You can re-analyze with more context for better results. The app is a tool to support your thinking, not a replacement for your own understanding of the situation.
Can this help me in workplace situations? ‚ñº
Yes. Linguistic Decoder can help you understand work emails, colleague communication, and workplace dynamics. However, for serious workplace issues like harassment, discrimination, or legal concerns, you should consult with HR, an employment lawyer, or other professional resources. Linguistic Decoder is a tool for understanding communication, not for resolving serious workplace problems.
What are the communication techniques in the educational library? ‚ñº
Linguistic Decoder includes proven communication techniques including: Grey Rock Method (maintaining emotional boundaries), BIFF Response Strategy (Brief, Informative, Friendly, Firm), "I" Statements (expressing yourself without blame), Active Listening, Boundary Setting, and Digital Safety. These are educational tools to help you improve communication skills.
