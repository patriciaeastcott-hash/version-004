What you can do

Analyse any conversation and build a history of insights that grow more valuable over time.

🧠 Conversation analysis

Paste text from any source — SMS, email, Slack, WhatsApp. Get AI-powered insights in seconds.

  • Emotional tone identification
  • Hidden meanings revealed
  • Communication style analysis
  • Plain English translation

👤 Speaker profiles

Create profiles for people in your conversations. Track patterns and get personalised insights for each relationship.

  • Tag conversations by person
  • Track communication history
  • Relationship type settings
  • Pattern recognition over time

💬 Response suggestions

Get AI-generated response options tailored to your situation — diplomatic, assertive, or collaborative.

  • Multiple response styles
  • Impact prediction
  • Context-aware suggestions

📚 Educational library

Access expert communication techniques including Grey Rock, BIFF, and "I" statements — works offline.

  • Proven communication strategies
  • Available offline
  • Learn at your own pace

📈 Historical tracking

Build a history of analyses. See trends in your communication over weeks and months.

  • Timeline view by person
  • Relationship health scores
  • Communication improvement tracking

🔒 Privacy first

All data stored locally on your device. We never store your conversations on our servers.

  • Local storage only
  • No server-side logs
  • Your privacy guaranteed

Who it's for

Whether it's work emails, family texts, or relationship chats — understand what's really being said.

Workplace communication

Decode passive-aggressive emails, understand office dynamics, and craft better professional responses.

Family relationships

Navigate complex family dynamics. Understand what's really being said (and not said) in family chats.

Romantic relationships

Build better communication patterns. Understand your partner's communication style and improve together.

Neurodivergent communication

If you struggle to read between the lines, let AI help decode the subtext that others seem to understand intuitively.
