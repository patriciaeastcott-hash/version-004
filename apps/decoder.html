Linguistic Decoder

Linguistic Decoder is the current flagship Digital ABCs beta. It helps users analyse written conversations for communication patterns, emotional tone, and relationship dynamics while being explicit about beta limits, AI limits, and the need for human judgment.

See beta access Open support

Status at a glance

  • Available now in public beta iOS and Android testers can access the current build through TestFlight and Firebase App Distribution.
  • Built for conversation analysis The product is intended to help users inspect patterns and possible meaning, not to provide certainty or advice.
  • Trust pages are part of the product surface Privacy, AI transparency, terms, child notices, and regional notices are linked directly from this page.
What the beta does

The current beta is designed for careful pattern inspection.

The product direction is focused on helping people slow down, inspect wording more clearly, and recognise patterns that may be hard to see in the moment.

Analyse written conversations

Paste or review text-based conversations to explore tone, possible subtext, and communication patterns.

Support reflection, not certainty

The output is meant to help users think more clearly, not to replace lived context, judgment, or professional advice.

Stay explicit about limits

The beta surfaces its support page and trust pages clearly because honest framing is part of the product standard.

Important limits

What Linguistic Decoder is not

The product page needs to keep the limits visible so the beta is not mistaken for a finished or professional-grade service.

Not medical or mental health advice

The beta can support reflection, but it does not diagnose, treat, or replace qualified health support.

Not legal or professional advice

The output should not be relied on for contracts, disputes, employment matters, or other professional decisions.

Not a high-risk safety tool

If a situation involves abuse, self-harm, immediate danger, or urgent welfare issues, do not rely on a beta AI app.

Use the beta with the trust pages open.

Linguistic Decoder should be read together with its support surface and the Digital ABCs trust pages. That is how the product explains data handling, AI use, child contexts, and regional notices without overclaiming certainty.
