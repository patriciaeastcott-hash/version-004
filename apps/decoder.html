Frequently Asked Questions

What is Expo Go and why do I need it?

Expo Go is a free app that lets you run development versions of mobile apps without installing them permanently. It's the standard way to test apps during development. Simply download Expo Go from the App Store or Google Play, then scan the QR code or open the link to run Linguistic Decoder.

Is my conversation data secure?

Yes. All your conversation data is stored locally on your device only. We do not store your conversations on any server. The AI analysis is processed securely, but the conversation content is never saved by us. You have full control over your data.

How do I create and update speaker profiles?

In the app, go to the Speaker Profiles section. Tap "Add New Speaker" to create a profile. Enter the speaker's name (or nickname) and select your relationship type (work colleague, family member, friend, partner, etc.). You can update profiles anytime by tapping on them and editing the details. Profiles help the AI provide more accurate, personalised analysis.

The app isn't loading. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of Expo Go installed
  2. Check your internet connection - Expo requires internet to load the app
  3. Close Expo Go completely and reopen it
  4. Try scanning the QR code again or use the direct link
  5. If on iOS, try opening the link in Safari instead of scanning

Still having issues? Email us at info@digitalabcs.com.au

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! As a beta tester, your feedback is invaluable. Please email your bug reports or feature suggestions to info@digitalabcs.com.au with the subject line "Linguistic Decoder Beta Feedback". Include:

  • Your device type (iPhone/Android) and OS version
  • What you were doing when the issue occurred
  • Screenshots if possible
Will my data be saved when the app updates?

During the beta testing phase via Expo Go, data persistence between sessions may vary. We recommend not relying on the app for permanent storage during testing. Once the app is officially released on the App Store/Google Play, your data will be properly saved on your device.

Is this app a replacement for professional help?

No. Linguistic Decoder is an educational tool designed to help you understand conversations better. It is not a substitute for professional mental health services, legal advice, relationship counselling, or medical diagnosis. If you're experiencing serious relationship issues, mental health concerns, or need professional guidance, please seek help from qualified professionals.

When will the full app be available?

We're currently in beta testing to refine the app based on real user feedback. The full release on the App Store and Google Play is planned for later this year. Beta testers will be notified when the official version launches and may receive special early-adopter benefits.
