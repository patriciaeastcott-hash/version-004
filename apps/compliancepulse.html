CompliancePulse

Compliance monitoring meets market intelligence. Continuously scans for risks while watching for opportunities - so you can stop playing catch-up.

The Problem

Small businesses get blindsided. A compliance requirement changes and suddenly you're facing fines. A grant opens up but you miss the deadline because who has time to monitor government websites? Regulations shift, opportunities appear and disappear - and you're too busy running your actual business to track it all.

Big companies have compliance teams. You have... yourself. And maybe a spreadsheet you stopped updating six months ago.

What It Does

Two engines running in the background while you focus on your business.

Risk Engine

Continuously monitors your business against ISO and GDPR requirements. Flags gaps before they become fines. Gives you a clear risk score so you know where to focus.

  • ISO compliance gap analysis
  • GDPR/Australian Privacy Act checks
  • Risk scoring and prioritisation
  • Actionable recommendations

Opportunity Engine

Watches government feeds, news sources, and regulatory updates. Alerts you to grants, tenders, and policy changes that affect your industry.

  • Government tender monitoring
  • Grant opportunity alerts
  • Regulatory change tracking
  • Industry news filtering

Local-First Data

All processing happens locally. Your business data never leaves your infrastructure. No cloud storage, no third-party access, no data sovereignty issues.

  • 100% local data processing
  • No cloud dependencies
  • Australian Privacy Act compliant
  • Your data stays yours
