CompliancePulse

Compliance monitoring meets market intelligence. Continuously scan for ISO/GDPR risks while watching government feeds for grants, tenders, and regulatory changes.

Secondary project

The problem it solves

Small businesses get blindsided by compliance issues and miss opportunities because they can't monitor everything. CompliancePulse automates the watching so you don't have to.

Planned features

  • Compliance scanning: Automated checks against ISO, GDPR, and industry-specific requirements
  • Government feed monitoring: Grants, tenders, and regulatory changes relevant to your business
  • Risk alerts: Get notified before problems become crises
  • Opportunity alerts: Never miss a grant or tender deadline again

This is a secondary project in the Digital ABCs portfolio. Our primary focus is on self-quantification apps like Linguistic Decoder, Mood Tracker, and Decision Journal.

If you're interested in CompliancePulse, get in touch and let us know. User interest helps us prioritise development.
