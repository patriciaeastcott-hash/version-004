About Sleep Cycles

A sleep cycle lasts about 90 minutes. Waking up at the end of a cycle helps you feel more refreshed. The average adult needs 5-6 complete cycles (7.5-9 hours) per night.

Recommended Sleep by Age

Newborn (0-3 months)14-17 hrs
Infant (4-11 months)12-15 hrs
Toddler (1-2 years)11-14 hrs
Preschool (3-5 years)10-13 hrs
School Age (6-13)9-11 hrs
Teen (14-17 years)8-10 hrs
Adult (18-64 years)7-9 hrs
Older Adult (65+)7-8 hrs
