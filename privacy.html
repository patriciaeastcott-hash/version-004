Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Digital ABCs and Beach to Bridge to Bush Logistics ("BBB Logistics") collect, use, and protect your personal information. Both businesses are operated by the same sole trader, based in Toongabbie, NSW, Australia.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What This Policy Covers

  • Digital ABCs: Website, mobile applications (iOS/Android), app development services, and tech consulting
  • BBB Logistics: Delivery bookings, client accounts, and logistics services

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for our business functions. What we collect depends on which service you use.

Digital ABCs - App Development & Tech Services

Information Type Examples Why We Collect It
Contact Details Name, email, phone, business name To communicate about projects and services
Project Information Business processes, system access, workflow data To deliver consulting and development services
Payment Information Billing address (card details handled by Stripe) To process payments for services
App Usage Data Content you submit to apps, feature usage, device info To provide app functionality and improve services

BBB Logistics - Delivery Services

Information Type Examples Why We Collect It
Contact Details Name, phone, email, business name To confirm bookings and communicate about deliveries
Address Information Pickup addresses, delivery addresses To complete deliveries and calculate pricing
Delivery Details Description of goods, special instructions, recipient names To handle goods appropriately and complete handovers
Payment Information Billing details, ABN (for business clients) To invoice and process payments

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our apps, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Location Data: Approximate location (city/region) from IP address - not precise GPS

This is collected via Google Analytics and helps us understand how people use our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Digital ABCs Uses

  • Deliver app development, consulting, and support services
  • Operate and improve our mobile and web applications
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Communicate about projects, updates, and support
  • Provide AI-powered features within our apps (see Section 5)

BBB Logistics Uses

  • Complete delivery bookings and coordinate pickups/drop-offs
  • Navigate to addresses using GPS/mapping services
  • Process payments and maintain delivery records
  • Communicate about delivery status and scheduling
  • Maintain records for insurance and compliance purposes

General Uses (Both Businesses)

  • Comply with legal obligations (tax records, safety requirements)
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • Protect against fraud and misuse of services
  • Improve our services based on aggregate usage patterns

5. AI Features and Third-Party AI

Some of our applications use AI-powered features (e.g., Linguistic Decoder's conversation analysis). Here's how we handle your data:

  • Third-Party Processing: Content you submit to AI features is sent to Google Gemini's API for processing
  • No Training: We do not use your content to train AI models. We configure API access to opt-out of training where available
  • Consent: Before using AI features, you'll be informed that content will be processed by third-party AI
  • Minimal Data: We only send the content necessary for the feature to work

See our AI Transparency Statement for more details.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password practices and access controls
  • Regular security updates to systems
  • Limited access - only those who need information to do their job

For BBB Logistics, physical delivery records (if any) are stored securely and disposed of appropriately.

No system is 100% secure. If a data breach occurs that is likely to cause serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the OAIC as required by law.

7. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Project files (Digital ABCs) Duration of project + 2 years Support and warranty obligations
Financial records 7 years Australian tax law requirements
Delivery records (BBB Logistics) 2 years Insurance and dispute resolution
App account data Until account deletion requested Service provision
Analytics data 26 months (Google Analytics default) Service improvement

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or de-identify it.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand site usage
  • Form Cookies: Tally.so for contact forms

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

Access Your Information

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. We'll respond within 30 days.

Correct Your Information

Request that we update or correct inaccurate information.

Request Deletion

Ask us to delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).

Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

Make a Complaint

If you believe we've breached the Privacy Act, contact us first. If unsatisfied with our response, you can complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@digitalabcs.com.au.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made. For significant changes, we'll provide notice through our website or direct communication.
